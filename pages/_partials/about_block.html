About echoIA

echoIA (Enabling Cosmology with Homogenized Observations of Intrinsic Alignments) is a community effort to build a unified pipeline for Intrinsic Alignment (IA) analyses. Our first step is simple but powerful: collect publicly available galaxy shape catalogs in one place with clean links and minimal, comparable metadata so everyone can quickly see what’s out there.

Shape catalogs provide per-galaxy shape measurements (e.g., ellipticities/shear) plus basic context like redshifts and flags. We don’t host data—each catalog stays at its original home (often a DOI on Zenodo or a survey repository)—we just make discovery fast and comparisons fair.

  • Compare: consistent, high-level fields across releases
